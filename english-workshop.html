"How to Talk So Kids Will Listen & Listen So Kids Will Talk"

The complete Faber & Mazlish programme, delivered entirely in English — for English-speaking families living in France or abroad.

About the workshop

Based on the bestselling book How to Talk So Kids Will Listen & Listen So Kids Will Talk by Adèle Faber and Elaine Mazlish, this 7-session programme gives you practical communication tools to build a more cooperative, respectful relationship with your children and teenagers.

The workshop covers the same topics as the French programme, entirely in English: acknowledging feelings, encouraging cooperation, alternatives to punishment, fostering independence, building self-esteem, and freeing children from roles and labels.

Format

  • 7 sessions of 2.5 to 3 hours each
  • Small groups (max. 8 participants) or individual sessions
  • Online (video call) or in person near Milhac-de-Nontron (Dordogne)
  • A workbook is provided at the first session (€15)
  • Recommended reading: How to Talk So Kids Will Listen & Listen So Kids Will Talk
